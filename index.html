  1. Leadership in Action:

    My directorial journey was marked by several pivotal projects that shaped the technological landscape of the organizations I served. Here are a few highlights:

    • Multi-Tenant SaaS Web Application:

      I'm currently spearheading the creation of this innovative tool aimed at revolutionizing operations for online shops. The vision for this project encompasses integrating with e-commerce giants like Amazon and Shopify, establishing robust end-to-end operations management from inventory control to production scheduling, and forging collaborations with shipping companies for seamless in-app label purchasing.

    • Digital Transformation at ABC Aluminum Solutions:

      Under my leadership, we achieved a significant 80% digital transformation. We deployed platforms like G-Suite, Salesforce, and the Atlassian Stack, not to mention intricate ERP-EDI integrations. Orchestrating network architecture, ensuring 99% uptime across facilities, and rolling out cloud infrastructure for our in-house micro-services were among the key accomplishments.
      Cloud-Based Field Service Ops Platform at Southwest Manufacturing: Spearheading the deployment of this platform transformed our data accessibility for work orders. Coupled with automation, it revamped our invoicing process, making it more efficient and streamlined.

  2. A Developer by Passion:

    While these leadership experiences were formative, my heart always yearned to dive deeper into the technical realm. Outside the confines of organizational strategy, I delved into the world of development. Every line of code was an exploration, every deployment a step closer to understanding the developer's universe from the inside out.
